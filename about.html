About The website :

We are kind of the people who live at the beautiful city of kuala lumpur and love running. The city of KL dose have a lot of magnificent parks and Hidden gems running trails and a lot of runners. We created this website to be a place for the runners community in KL to gather and share their experiences. We will be exploring running trails in the city as much as we can and will be reviewing them and we hope other runners will contribute with their own reviews. Also We will be posting running-related videos and articles that we like.
